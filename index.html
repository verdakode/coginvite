◈ MANIFESTO ◈

The question of labor automation has never been so top of mind. As some of the first to ever say it, we at Cognition have been thinking about what comes after. We believe that "units of labor" previously thought of as human software engineers can now be delegated powerfully, but the world has not yet caught up with how to do this.

While thinking about the way we think, the ancient technology of cartography came to mind as a compression technology that makes it easier to take action. While the map is most certainly not the territory, Codemaps has made it easier than ever for us to delegate effectively, turning every person into a CEO.

Our new era of metacognition reflects the data we have gathered over the past few years. We hope you will join us to think a bit about thinking.
